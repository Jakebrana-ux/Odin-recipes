PIZZA

Authenic Neopolitan Pizza

This recipe brings the taste of Italy straight to your kitchen. Fresh cheese and basil top a rustic tomato sauce that blankets an airy and flakey crust.

INGREDIENTS

  • 00 Wheat Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pureed Tomato Sauce
  • Sun Dried Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil

STEPS

  1. The night before you want to make the piza, prepare the dough - mix 640 grams of flour, 360grams of water, 14 grams of salt and .3 grams of yeast together in a bowl.
  2. Once the dough no longer sticks to the bowl move it to a covered container and proof for 1 hour. After 1 hour remove the dough and knead it back into a ball and proof overnight. In the morning remove the dough, knead it and cut it into 4 equal parts and roll them into balls. Place the balls back in the container and continue proofing until you are ready to make the pizza.
  3. For the sauce, simmer the pureed and sun dried tomatoes on medium-low heat until they reach your desired thickness, add salt and pepper to flavor.
  4. Preheat oven to its' highest temperature, usually 500 degrees for domestic ovens
  5. Place the dough balls onto a plate of flour and press the middle outward until a thick outer crust is formed, continue to stretch outward until a roughly 10 inch pizza is formed
  6. Spread your sauce across the top of the dough until you can no longer see the dough beneath it
  7. Top with the mozzerella, parmesan, basil, salt and pepper. Drizzle olive oil across the toppings.
  8. Cook at highest oven temperature (usually 500) for about 6-8 minutes, preferrably on a pizza stone.
  9. Top with any extra parmesan, salt or pepper to taste. Let it cool, then enjoy an authentic taste of Italy!
